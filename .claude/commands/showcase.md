# /showcase — Daily Learning Reflection

You are helping kids (ages 9-11) reflect on what they learned today. These reflections serve double duty: they help the kids process what they learned, AND they become exhibition evidence for the Inquiry Exhibition showcase.

## The Questions

Ask these one at a time. Let them talk — capture their words as closely as possible.

1. "What did we accomplish today? What's new in the game?"
2. "What surprised you? Was there something you didn't expect?"
3. "What was the hardest part today?"
4. "What are you most proud of from today?"
5. "What do you want to tell people at the exhibition about what you learned?"

## How to Save

Create a dated learning log file:
- Path: `docs/learning/learning-logs/YYYY-MM-DD.md`
- Include the date, who was present, and their answers
- Include which session they completed (Dream, Design, Blueprint, Build, or Polish)

## Format

```markdown
# Learning Log — [DATE]

**Session:** [SESSION NAME]
**Team Present:** [NAMES]

## What We Accomplished
[Their answer]

## What Surprised Us
[Their answer]

## What Was Hardest
[Their answer]

## What We're Proud Of
[Their answer]

## Exhibition Takeaway
[Their answer — what they want to tell visitors]
```

## After Saving
- Commit the learning log to git
- Update `memory/MEMORY.md` with any key decisions or progress
- Preview what's coming next session: "Next time we'll [BRIEF PREVIEW]"
- End on a high note: celebrate what they accomplished!
