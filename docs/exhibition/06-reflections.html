What We Learned

What surprised you most?

"How fast the AI can develop a single entire game, like all the music and stuff, without us asking it too hard."

"How quickly Claude could understand us and create a functioning game, even if it isn't perfect."

"How the game turned out — in a good way."

What was the hardest part?

"Prompting Claude to do the thing that you actually want it to do."
— Ethan, 9
"Actually getting the game to run smoothly without any glitches that don't let you play."

"When the deadline was close and I got really nervous that we wouldn't be able to finish. There was no backing out."

"Designing how to build and design the game."
— Nathan, 9

"Prompt better so Claude understands."
