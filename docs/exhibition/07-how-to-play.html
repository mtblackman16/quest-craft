How to Play

Nintendo Switch Pro Controller

You're a jello cube trapped in a dark castle. Fight enemies with your own body, craft health, dodge attacks, and escape floor by floor to the rooftop. 15 floors. 5 bosses. 1 life.

Controls

Left Stick / D-Pad Move
A Jump
B Jelly Shot(costs health!)
X Eat / Heal
Y Interact
Stick Down Ground Pound
ZL Split / Combine
ZR Perfect Dodge
+ Pause
Your health IS your ammo
Ground pound = strongest attack
Look for hidden doors
You die, you restart
