How to Play

Nintendo Switch Pro Controller
ButtonAction
Left Stick / D-PadMove left and right
A ButtonJump
B ButtonJelly Shot (shoot a glob of jello — costs health!)
X ButtonEat Jello Powder (heal)
Y ButtonInteract (talk to NPCs, use objects)
Stick DownGround Pound (slam down while in the air)
ZR ButtonPerfect Dodge (quick invincibility)
ZL ButtonSplit into pieces / Recombine
+ ButtonPause / Inventory

Tips

  • Your health IS your ammo — every shot costs jello mass. Don't shoot too much!
  • Find jello powder, water, and cooking pots to craft health refills
  • Ground pound is your strongest attack — try it on groups of enemies
  • Look for hidden doors and secret passages
  • If you die, you start over — be careful!
