SPLIT

You're a jello cube. You're trapped. The only way out is UP.

The Story

You're a jello cube with eyeballs, trapped in the dungeon of a dark castle in the desert. Fight monsters with your own jello body, solve hidden puzzles, craft health from jello ingredients, and escape floor by floor to the rooftop where the final boss waits.

Your Powers

  • Jelly Shot — fire globs of your own body (costs health!)
  • Ground Pound — slam down to crush enemies below
  • Split — break into smaller pieces to fit through gaps
  • Perfect Dodge — brief invincibility with precise timing
  • Craft — cook jello powder + water to heal
  • Elemental Pills — fire, ice, electricity, and more
