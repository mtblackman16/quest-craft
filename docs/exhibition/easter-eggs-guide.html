Secret Easter Eggs

Can you find all 8?
1
The Architect's Room
Ground-pound the cracked tile in your starting cell (Floor 1)
Hidden room with 4 colored jello cubes named ETHAN, EINS, ANDREW, and NATHAN, plus a wall-mounted key
2
Konami Jello
Enter the Konami Code on the title screen
Jello explodes, reforms, and a "Secrets: 1/?" counter appears on screen
3
Andrew's Gallery
Follow the purple-tinted torches on Floor 7
A hidden corridor with Andrew's original artwork displayed as framed paintings on castle walls
4
Fourth-Wall Fracture
Die exactly 4 times
The screen "glitches" and reboots with static and color separation, then golden jello powder appears in your starting cell
5
Claude the Shopkeeper
Find the hidden barrel on Floor 3 and interact with it 5 times
The barrel starts talking and eventually reveals: "My name is Claude. Yes, THAT Claude."
6
Jello Graveyard
Miss a lot of jello shots (automatic)
Missed shots leave permanent green splats on the walls. Get 50+ and they haunt you at the end
7
The Idle Dance
Stand completely still for 30 seconds
Your jello cube breaks into a dance with floating music notes while cooking pots bubble in rhythm
8
Exhibition Day Mode
Play on exhibition day (March 27, 2026)
Golden border around the screen, first enemy drops an "Exhibited" trophy that appears inside your jello body
SPLIT - 8 secrets hidden by Ethan, Eins, Andrew, and Nathan

How to Spot a Secret

🌿
Reactive Vines
Grow from the wall and retract when you get close. Hidden passage behind.
🔥
Fast-Flicker Torch
Torch flickers faster than normal. Draft from a hidden opening nearby.
💚
Green-Flame Torch
Rare green fire. Matches your jello color -- left here for YOU.
💀
Extinguished Torch
Empty bracket, no flame. Danger ahead -- lots of enemies.
〰️
Jello Wobble
You wobble more than usual. Something hidden is nearby to interact with.
🔊
Sound Shift
Subtle low hum. You're within 3 tiles of a hidden room.
