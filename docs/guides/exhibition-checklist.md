## The Demo Script

Practice this flow:

1. **Greeting:** "Hi! We're [TEAM NAME] and we built a game called [GAME NAME]!"
2. **The Pitch:** "It's a [GENRE] game where you [MAIN ACTION]. The coolest part is [HOOK]."
3. **How We Built It:** "We designed the whole game by talking to an AI called Claude. We told it what we wanted, and it helped us build it. We drew all the art ourselves."
4. **Invite to Play:** "Want to try it? Here are the controls: [QUICK EXPLANATION]."
5. **While They Play:** Stand by to help. Point out cool features. Be proud!
6. **The Closer:** "The hardest part was [X]. The thing we're most proud of is [Y]."

### Who Does What
- [ ] **Person 1:** Greets visitors, gives the pitch
- [ ] **Person 2:** Explains how the game was built (points to display)
- [ ] **Person 3:** Hands controllers to visitors, explains controls
- [ ] **Person 4:** Technical support (restarts game if needed, troubleshoots)
- Rotate roles every 30 minutes!
